# Contributing

We welcome contributions that improve:

- clarity of definitions  
- semantic consistency  
- interoperability with identity systems  
- threat modelling and misuse cases  

## Scope of Contributions

Contributions should focus on the **semantic layer**, including:

- capacity expression  
- authority referencing  
- mandate scoping  
- consent conditions  
- revocation pathways  

## Contribution Boundary

SILT Core defines a public semantic layer.

It may be discussed in external standards bodies and working groups.

However:

- Contributions in external forums do not constitute transfer of the full SILT architecture  
- SILT may include additional models and structures not disclosed publicly  
- This repository reflects the **public specification layer only**

Please avoid:

- platform-specific assumptions  
- collapsing capacity into identity or permissions  
- removing revocation logic  

Before major contributions, open an issue for discussion.
