# SILT Positioning

SILT Core defines a missing semantic layer in digital systems.

Most systems answer:
- who is acting
- what they can prove
- what they are allowed to do

SILT answers:
- in what capacity an actor is acting  
- under what authority  
- with what scope  
- whether that authority is valid and revocable  

## Stack Role

SILT sits between identity/credential layers and execution systems.

It acts as a **legitimacy precondition** for action.

## Contribution Model

In external forums:

- SILT contributes problem framing and semantic requirements  
- SILT does not transfer its full internal architecture  

SILT remains an independently developed authority-layer architecture.
